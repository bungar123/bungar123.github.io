Thermo Fisher Scientific – LPG Data Governance & Alation Data Catalog

LP Data Governance Analyst Intern II · May 2024 – Aug 2024 · Carlsbad, CA (On-site)

Alation Data Governance Data Catalog Metadata Management Supply Chain Stakeholder Enablement Excel (staging)

Problem

Supply Chain stakeholders needed a clear, trusted way to discover and interpret LPG product data. Critical attributes and definitions were spread across systems, making it hard to identify the “right” fields, compare definitions, and use consistent metrics across reporting.

What I Did

  • Built and maintained an LPG data catalog glossary by mapping product attributes to their source systems.
  • Standardized definitions and reorganized attribute groupings to improve usability and reduce ambiguity.
  • Coordinated with colleagues to publish and operationalize the glossary inside Alation for stakeholder self-service.
  • Improved accessibility and navigation so stakeholders could quickly find definitions, context, and usage guidance.

About Alation

Alation is a data catalog and governance platform that helps organizations scale data initiatives by making metadata searchable, reusable, and easier to understand—supporting analytics and enabling new AI initiatives.

Approach

  • Inventory the attributes most used by Supply Chain reporting and analysis.
  • Define & standardize names, definitions, and grouping logic for consistent interpretation.
  • Map each attribute to its source system and document key context for correct usage.
  • Publish in Alation to enable fast search, discovery, and stakeholder self-service.
  • Iterate with feedback to ensure the catalog matched real stakeholder needs and questions.

Deliverables

  • Attribute glossary and data dictionary (standardized definitions + structured groupings).
  • Source-system mappings for product attributes to improve traceability and discovery.
  • Published Alation catalog content for stakeholder self-service and consistent reporting.

Impact

  • Improved data discovery by giving stakeholders a single place to search and understand LPG attributes.
  • Reduced confusion and inconsistent reporting through standardized definitions and clearer grouping structure.
  • Increased adoption by publishing through Alation and improving navigation/accessibility.
