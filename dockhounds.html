Lake Country DockHounds – Hitter/Position Player Scouting Reports

Baseball Analytics / Scouting Support · May 2023 – Sep 2023

Baseball Analytics Video Data Collection Excel Data Cleaning Reporting Player/Coach Communication

Problem

Hitters and coaches needed clear, actionable information on opposing starting pitchers—delivered quickly and consistently—so they could plan approaches by count and batter handedness.

What I Did

  • Mined pitch-level data from game film using a dual-monitor workflow (video + structured logging sheet).
  • Captured details including pitch type, velocity, location, count, pitch outcome, and in-play results.
  • Cleaned and summarized the data into tendencies and percent breakdowns (RHB, LHB, and overall).
  • Produced concise daily scouting reports and presented findings in pre-game meetings with players/staff.

Workflow

1) Video → Data Capture
  • Log each pitch event into a structured sheet
  • Standardize categories (pitch types, outcomes)
2) Cleaning → Analysis
  • Validate entries and resolve inconsistencies
  • Compute usage + outcome rates by count & handedness
3) Reporting
  • Generate quick hitter-facing takeaways
  • Include percentage tendencies & simple visuals
4) Communication
  • Deliver report + talk through key points pre-game
  • Answer hitter questions and clarify approach notes

Key Outputs

  • RHB vs LHB tendencies: pitch mix, location patterns, and go-to pitches in leverage counts
  • Count-based strategy: what shows up in 0–0, 1–0, 0–2, 3–2, etc.
  • Efficiency flags: time-to-pick-up cues (pace, patterns) and pitch sequencing notes
  • One-page summary: designed for fast digestion in a pre-game setting

Impact

  • Enabled hitters to enter games with a structured plan based on the opponent’s tendencies.
  • Improved day-to-day preparation through consistent reporting and direct communication.
  • Built a repeatable process for turning raw video into actionable insights under time constraints.
