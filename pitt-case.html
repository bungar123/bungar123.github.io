DICK’S Sporting Goods Strategy Case

Pitt Business Strategy Case Competition · Top 8 National Finalist

Business Strategy Retail Analytics Customer Loyalty Personalization Merchandise Strategy KPIs Financial Feasibility Executive Presentation

Summary

Selected as a Top 8 National Finalist in the 2025 Pitt Business / DICK’S Sporting Goods Strategy Case Competition. Worked on a multi-round, data-informed strategy case focused on strengthening in-store experiences, improving merchandise attachment, and building long-term Athlete loyalty in DICK’S House of Sport locations.

My Role

  • Served as a primary organizer for the team by coordinating meetings, aligning timelines, and ensuring deliverables were completed on schedule.
  • Helped lead exploratory analysis to understand relationships between Athlete engagement, experience participation, and purchasing behavior.
  • Contributed to the overall business strategy by shaping recommendations around experience utilization, loyalty, personalization, and merchandise alignment.
  • Played a key role in translating analytical insights into clear, executive-ready narratives for presentation.
  • Supported feasibility and justification discussions during later rounds, helping frame why proposed initiatives were realistic and investable.

Approach (High Level)

  • Evaluated how in-store experiences could be better utilized and connected to merchandise sales.
  • Developed frameworks to strengthen repeat engagement and improve Athlete loyalty over time.
  • Considered personalization strategies to enhance the end-to-end in-store journey.
  • Assessed operational feasibility and measurement approaches to ensure recommendations were actionable.

What Made This Competitive

  • Clear linkage between experience engagement and business outcomes.
  • Structured KPIs to measure utilization, conversion, retention, and long-term impact.
  • Balanced focus on innovation, feasibility, and execution.
  • Strong team coordination and presentation clarity under time constraints.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Business strategy and structured problem solving
  • Retail and customer analytics (high-level)
  • Loyalty and personalization strategy design
  • Executive communication and storytelling
  • Feasibility and investment justification
  • Team leadership and coordination
